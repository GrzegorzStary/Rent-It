Frequently Asked Questions

Rent It is a peer-to-peer rental marketplace where owners can list items for rent and renters can book them for a fee. Once the owner accepts the request, payment is made through the platform, and both parties arrange pickup or delivery. After the rental period, the item is returned and reviews can be left.

Anyone 18+ with a valid email, phone number, and payment method can use Rent It. Businesses can also list items as long as they comply with local laws and our terms.

Browse or search for an item, request a booking for your desired dates, wait for the owner to confirm, pay through the platform, arrange pickup/delivery, use the item, then return it and leave a review.

After signing up, go to “Create listing” fill in the details, upload photos, set a price and availability, then publish it. Your item will appear in search results for renters.

Payments are handled securely through Rent It. Renters pay when the booking is confirmed, and owners receive payment after the rental ends, minus our small service fee.

Renters are responsible for returning items in the same condition they received them. If an item is damaged, owners can report the issue and request compensation through Rent It. Compensation is provided up to the deposit amount specified by the owner during the listing process. Additionally, renters have the option to purchase their own third-party insurance for extra protection.
